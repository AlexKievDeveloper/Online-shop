The proverb of the pen being mightier than the sword could not be truer, especially considering how these pens are made from the wood of a 250-year gunship! Made from the residual timber sourced from the HMS Victory, a 104-gun British warship used in the Battle of Trafalgar in 1805, the pen was created by Peter Sandford, a UK-based maker who managed to get his hands on the precious wood when the gunship was being renovated.
